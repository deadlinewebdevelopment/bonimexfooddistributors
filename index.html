About Bonimex

Founded in 2001, Bonimex Food Distributors is built on a story of hard work, family, and growth. The owner came to the United States from Mexico in 1991 as a young man, working alongside his brothers.

What began with a single car grew into a van, then a larger vehicle, and eventually a full-size truck. Today, Bonimex continues to deliver quality produce with pride and reliability.
